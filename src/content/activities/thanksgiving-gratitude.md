## Activity Instructions

1. **Introduction (5 minutes)**
   - Sit in a circle with your family
   - Explain that Thanksgiving is about being grateful (感恩节 - gǎn'ēn jié)
   - Practice saying "我很感谢..." (wǒ hěn gǎnxiè... - I am grateful for...)

2. **Vocabulary Practice (10 minutes)**
   - Go through each vocabulary word together
   - Have children repeat after you
   - Point to family members and practice saying 家人 (jiārén)

3. **Gratitude Sharing (10 minutes)**
   - Each family member shares one thing they're grateful for in Mandarin
   - Start with the phrase "我很感谢..."
   - Parents can help younger children
   - Everyone responds with "谢谢" (xièxie)

4. **Art Activity (Optional)**
   - Draw or write what you're grateful for on cards
   - Label drawings with Chinese characters (with help)

## Tips for Parents

- Keep it simple and fun
- Don't worry about perfect pronunciation
- Celebrate every attempt to speak Mandarin
- You can do this activity in both English and Mandarin

## Cultural Connection

In Chinese culture, expressing gratitude and respect for family is deeply valued. While China doesn't celebrate Thanksgiving, the concept of 感恩 (gǎn'ēn - gratitude) is very important in Chinese tradition.
