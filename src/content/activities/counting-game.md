## Activity Instructions

1. **Setup**
   - Find an open space (living room, backyard, or park)
   - No materials needed!

2. **How to Play**
   - Call out a number in Mandarin (1-10)
   - Your child jumps that many times
   - Count together in Mandarin as they jump

3. **Numbers to Practice**
   - 一 (yī) - 1
   - 二 (èr) - 2
   - 三 (sān) - 3
   - 四 (sì) - 4
   - 五 (wǔ) - 5
   - 六 (liù) - 6
   - 七 (qī) - 7
   - 八 (bā) - 8
   - 九 (jiǔ) - 9
   - 十 (shí) - 10

4. **Variations**
   - Clap instead of jump
   - Spin around
   - Hop on one foot
   - Take steps forward/backward

## Tips for Parents

- Start with 1-5 for younger children
- Make it silly and fun
- Take turns - let your child call out numbers too
- Repeat, repeat, repeat!
